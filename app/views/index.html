What is levy transfer matching?

Levy transfer matching allows levy-paying employers to transfer their unused levy funds to other businesses to pay for apprenticeship training and assessment.

The apprenticeship service lets levy payers publicly advertise pledges (pots of funding) so that any other business can apply to them for funding. Levy payers can attach optional criteria to their pledges so they can make transfers to the sectors or geographical locations etc that they want to support.

The service for levy payers is called 'Pledge and transfer your levy funds' and sits within their employer account in 'Manage apprenticeships'. The publicly available service open to any business to see transfer funding opportunities and apply to pledges is called 'Find a business to fund apprenticeship training'.

All documents and links are being collected on a Confluence page.

This documents the Discovery, Alpha and Beta phases of the levy transfer matching project from January 2021 onwards.
