The yearly estimate costs are based on the planned start date of training and the 20% completion payment. It uses the financial year starting April. For our forecast we assume the completion payment will be made in the month after the planned end date.

How is my transfer allowance calculated?

The transfer allowance is an estimate because it is based on information that can change in the financial year.

These factors in your transfer funding application can vary: start and end dates, final agreed costs, and breaks in learning. Therefore this estimate can only be used as a guide.
